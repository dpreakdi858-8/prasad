TASK 7: Jeera Rice Recipe

To make Jeera Rice, the required ingredients are listed below, and follow the steps.

INGREDIENTS
  • Rice (1 cup – basmati preferred)
  • Jeera (cumin seeds)
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Green chilli (optional)
  • Bay leaf
  • Cloves (2)
  • Cinnamon (small piece)

STEPS

  1. Wash the rice and soak it for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in a pan.
  3. Add jeera and let it crackle.
  4. Add bay leaf, cloves and cinnamon.
  5. Add water (2 cups) and salt.
  6. When water starts boiling, add soaked rice.
  7. Cook on medium flame until rice becomes soft.
  8. Turn off the flame and rest for 5 minutes.
  9. Fluff the rice gently.
  10. Jeera Rice is ready to serve!
